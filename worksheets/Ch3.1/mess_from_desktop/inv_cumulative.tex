The random variable $Z$ is normally distributed such that $\mu=0$ and $\sigma=1$. 
$$Z\sim \N{0}{1} $$
We have introduced the cumulative function, $\Phi(z)$.
$$\Phi(z) ~=~ P(Z < z) ~=~ \text{Left area under density function up to }z $$
